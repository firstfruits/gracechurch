Everyone Reaching One To Reach One More

We welcome all to our church services. If good bible teaching is important to you, then please come and visit us. As the Wellington congregation of the Grace Presbyterian Church of New Zealand, we are dedicated to honouring God and His Word, and reaching the region with the Good News of Salvation through Jesus Christ. As a church we like to share in fellowship before and after our church service, so please don’t hesitate to come early and share a coffee with us! We’re a diverse, easy going bunch and like meeting new people. Children are very welcome, and a Sunday school is provided for a range of ages.
